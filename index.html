How to share Google Drive images:
  1. Upload your images to Google Drive
  2. Create a folder or select existing folder with images
  3. Right-click on folder → "Share"
  4. Change permissions to "Anyone with the link can view"
  5. Copy link and paste above
  6. Images will be viewable directly in the marketplace
